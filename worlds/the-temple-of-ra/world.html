About this World

An ancient desert temple has been unearthed. Legends say it holds the treasure of the sun god Ra, but it is protected by deadly traps and mind-bending puzzles.

This is a single-player adventure map designed for Minecraft 1.20.1. It should take approximately 30-45 minutes to complete.

Rules:

  • Play in Adventure Mode
  • Set brightness to Moody
  • No cheating (obviously!)
